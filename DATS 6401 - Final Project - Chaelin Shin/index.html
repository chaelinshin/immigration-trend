Introduction

Immigration has been a hot topic recently both worldwide and in the United States. We frequently hear news about countries experiencing an influx of migrants from other countries. Also, under the current U.S. administration, there have been many talks about changing various aspects of the U.S. immigration law. Thus, this project will look into the immigration trends, mainly focusing on the trends of the U.S.

Overview

First, this project will explore the flow of refugees worldwide, and how the number of refugees changed over the years. Then, this project will narrow down its scope and focus on the immigration trends of the U.S. It will look at the changes in numbers of selected immigrant (or non-immigrant) categories, and see whether the trends have changed over the years, especially, after the current president assumed office in 2017.
